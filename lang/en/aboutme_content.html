I'm Sarah, and my passion in life: photography (wildlife and landscapes) that I practice since few years around the world.

Let me explain how it started: Whereas I was a little girl, my dad given me his "Minolta" ... and the passion was born.

Wildlife parks and natural reserves have become over time a perfect terrain for training, and to discover a multitude of colors, movements and incredible scenery. Much more than a job, it's a passion!

The nature is also a growing source of inspiration: Tell me, who has never stopped in front of a beautiful sunset ? For me it is a stolen moment, on the edge of the nightlife, which offers a magical show ...

At night, there are the lights of the night , the shadows ... and everything takes a new face. It is quite disturbing, isn't it?

If one day this scenes will not inspire myself and invite me to capture the moment, there will be time for me to find a new passion. Until then, Welcome into my world ...
